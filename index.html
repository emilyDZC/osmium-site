What we do

Osmium is a flexible monitoring and control platform built for remote sites. Whether it’s wind turbines, modular buildings, or platform lighting, Osmium gives you real-time visibility of your assets, so you can diagnose issues, respond instantly, and avoid costly site visits. Our modular approach means you can start small, scale easily, and quickly prove value through a low-risk pilot.

How it works

1. Install: Fit lightweight Osmium hardware to your assets.

2. Connect: Link to existing systems with plug-and-play setup.

3. Monitor & Control: View real-time data and send remote commands from any device.

4. Optimise: Use insights to cut downtime, save costs, and extend equipment life.
