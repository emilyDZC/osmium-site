Energy Monitoring

You already know how much energy you are using each month. Wouldn't it be great to know where that energy is going, and when?
  • Real insights: Working with our electrical product supply partner Europa PLC, we help our customers see where energy is being used 24 hours a day. The insights we generate help our customers take action to reduce energy costs.
  • Bespoke solutions: We provide new smart single-phase and three-phase distribution boards where these are being replaced or fitted as part of a new build and we also offer retrofit solutions, leaving existing distribution boards in place. Our customers see real-time and historic (half-hourly daily, weekly etc) energy usage on our standard dashboard or if they wish on a bespoke dashboard configured to meet their unique requirements.
  • Isolate/restore: Where required we also provide 'isolate' and 'restore' buttons on our dashboard give managers the capability to temporarily stop energy from being used on a particular circuit at a particular time.
